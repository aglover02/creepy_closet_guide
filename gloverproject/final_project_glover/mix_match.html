Mix and Match

Use the arrows to browse through tops, bottoms, and shoes. Try changing one category at a time to see how the outfit shifts.

Styling Tips

  • Try changing only one category at a time to see what affects the outfit most.
  • Chunkier shoes can make an outfit feel heavier and more dramatic.
  • A fitted top with looser bottoms creates a different silhouette than an oversized top with slimmer bottoms.
  • Repeating dark colors can make mixed pieces feel more cohesive.
  • Even simple basics can feel more intentional when the proportions work together.

Try These Combos

  • Dark fitted top + plaid skirt + combat boots
  • Oversized top + cargo pants + chunky sneakers
  • Mesh top + wide-leg pants + platforms
  • Simple black top + dark jeans + heavy boots
  • Graphic top + loose pants + dark sneakers
